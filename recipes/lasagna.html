Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese. This hearty and flavorful recipe is a favorite for family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion, garlic, and herbs for seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef or Italian sausage in a pan.
  3. Add chopped onions, garlic, and herbs to the meat and cook until fragrant.
  4. Layer the lasagna: noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  5. Repeat the layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in the oven until bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
