Musaka

Description

Musaka, also known as Moussaka, is a traditional Mediterranean dish made with layers of eggplant, minced meat, and béchamel sauce. It's a delightful casserole-style dish that offers a rich and savory experience.

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onions and garlic
  • Tomatoes and tomato sauce
  • Potatoes
  • Flour and milk for béchamel sauce
  • Feta or Parmesan cheese
  • Herbs and spices for seasoning

Steps

  1. Slice and salt the eggplant to remove excess moisture.
  2. Brown the ground meat with onions and garlic.
  3. Add tomatoes, herbs, and spices to create the meat sauce.
  4. Layer sliced potatoes and eggplant in a baking dish.
  5. Spread the meat sauce over the vegetables.
  6. Prepare béchamel sauce and pour it over the layers.
  7. Top with crumbled feta or Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake until golden and bubbly.
